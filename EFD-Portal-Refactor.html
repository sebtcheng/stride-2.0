Interpretation

This hybrid design separates the dashboard into three clear layers. The knowledge layer explains the national portfolio: 12,381 projects, ₱9.84B in funds, regional distribution, and funding-year distribution. The monitoring layer tracks engineer progress reporting across regions, showing updated versus not-yet-updated projects. The strategic layer focuses leadership attention on the urgent goal: all FY 2026 project records must be updated by month end.
